Privacy Policy – FairSplitly

Effective Date: January 2026

FairSplitly respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the FairSplitly application.

1. Information Collection

FairSplitly does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, or contact details.

However, the app displays advertisements provided by third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob). These services may automatically collect limited non-personal information, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate (non-precise) location
  • Ad interaction data

This information is used solely for displaying and measuring advertisements, in accordance with the advertising provider’s privacy policies.

2. Advertisements & In-App Purchase

FairSplitly includes banner and interstitial advertisements.

Users may optionally remove all advertisements permanently through a one-time in-app purchase (“Remove Ads”).

When ads are removed, no advertisements are shown and no advertising-related data is processed.

3. Data Storage

All expense data, participant names, and calculations are stored locally on the user’s device only. This data is not transmitted to external servers.

4. Analytics & Tracking

FairSplitly does not use custom analytics tools, does not track users across apps or websites, and does not sell or share user data with third parties or data brokers.

Any advertising-related data processing is handled solely by the advertising provider and only when ads are enabled.

5. Permissions

FairSplitly does not request access to sensitive device features such as camera, microphone, contacts, or precise location.

6. Children’s Privacy

FairSplitly does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Advertisements are delivered in compliance with applicable child protection regulations.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app or on the App Store page.

8. Contact
