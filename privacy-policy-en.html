Privacy Policy for Candle – Focus & Pomodoro

Effective Date: September 2025

Candle – Focus & Pomodoro (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App, including the use of advertising services.

1. Data Collection

The App itself does not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location. However, for advertising purposes, third-party services (Google AdMob) may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Cookies and usage data related to ad interactions

2. Data Usage

- Local usage data (e.g., your focus sessions, timers) remains only on your device. - Advertising data collected by Google AdMob is used to serve and improve ads, and may be used for personalized or non-personalized advertising depending on your device settings.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for displaying ads. Google AdMob may collect and process data as described in Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not share user-created data with third parties. However, Google AdMob may share collected advertising data in accordance with their privacy practices.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. Google AdMob provides COPPA-compliant ads in child-directed apps. Parents can manage ad personalization in their device settings.

6. Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Advertising data is retained according to Google AdMob’s policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Effective Date”.
